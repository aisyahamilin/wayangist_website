ABOUT WAYANG KULIT

Introduction

Wayang Kulit is a traditional form of shadow puppetry widely practiced in Malaysia and Southeast Asia. It uses intricate leather puppets projected onto a screen with light and shadows. Accompanied by gamelan music, it brings to life mythological tales and folklore that reflect spiritual and cultural roots.

Purpose

Our aim is to digitally preserve, promote, and provide public access to the legacy of Wayang Kulit through archiving and cultural education.

Vision

To become a leading digital platform that preserves the tradition of Wayang Kulit and ensures its relevance for future generations.
